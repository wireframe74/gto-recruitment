Our History

The Australian Industry Group (Ai Group) is Australia's peak industry association. Acting on behalf of business for more than 140 years, we are the country's only truly national employers' organisation.

Ai Group represents the interests of more than 60,000 businesses employing more than 1 million staff. Our longstanding involvement with diverse industry sectors including manufacturing, construction, transport, labour hire, mining services, defence, airlines and ICT means we are genuinely representative of Australian industry. With more than 250 staff in offices across NSW, QLD, SA, VIC and WA, we have the resources and the expertise to meet the changing needs of our members.

We provide the practical information, advice and assistance you need to run your business more effectively. Ai Group also offers members a voice at all levels of government through our policy leadership and influence.

Our deep experience of industrial relations and workplace law positions Ai Group as the leading advocate on behalf of enterprises large and small across Australia. We intrinsically appreciate the challenges facing industry and remain at the cutting edge of policy debate and legislative change – and strategic business management.

Our Mission

Our Mission is what role we see we want Ai Group to play in realising our vision. We want to be Australian Industry’s strongest voice and most enduring partner.

Vision

Our Vision is what it is that we believe in and what we want Ai Group to make a difference in achieving.

Thriving industry for a prosperous community

Reflecting not just the importance we attach to the success of Australian industry but also our desire that its success helps to lift the well-being of the broader community in which we live.

Values

Our Values capture the ways we want to go about what we do.
We want to: Put our members front and centre of all that we do.
We seek to: Be connected, build relationships and be a team.
We want our people to: Pursue excellence, growth and learning.
And we will: Act with integrity, accountability and fairness.

Careers

Connected, flexible and sustainable, we ensure you'll have the opportunity to be involved in conversations and share your ideas.

Our workplaces provide opportunities to help fast track your learning and your success. A career at Ai Group could take you anywhere.

Why us

Diversity and Inclusion

We are a diverse bunch here at Ai Group – you’ll find individuals from varied backgrounds with different ways of thinking, different skills and different experiences. Ai Group understands the importance of diversity and inclusion – our long history of success has come from being different.

We harness the strength of our diversity through inclusion. Inclusion means creating a workplace environment where people feel they can be themselves, regardless of their gender, age, ethnicity or cultural affiliation, sexual orientation, beliefs or educational background.

Flexibility

We are big on being flexible whether it is offering differing start and finish times, part time hours or work from home we have you covered! Strong Connections – You will be working with some of Australia’s leading companies to help them be the best that they can be.

Through this your network will become unparalleled.

A path forward

We are focused on your success – we want our people to not only succeed but also grow through our commitment to learning and development.

A team shaping Australian Industry

We work hand in hand with industry to shape the future of the Australian economy – we support great working conditions and viable policies to help Australian businesses succeed every day. So you can be proud of the work that you do now and into the future

For 39 years, Ai Group has taken the time to understand our business so they give us the right advice, with everything from workplace issues to business reviews.

JAMIE BAENSCH

GENERAL MANAGER, AIR RADIATORS
